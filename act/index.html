Australian Capital Territory redistribution 2025

The redistribution of the Australian Capital Territory commenced on 12 August 2025. Public suggestions were sought from 8 October 2025 until 7 November 2925. Here you can find details of my suggestion to the Redistribution Committee.

Proposed electoral divisions

My suggestion ensures all electoral divisions in the Australian Capital Territory meet the numerical thresholds for actual and projected enrolment, while adhering to community of interests criteria as outlined in the Commonwealth Electoral Act 1918.
