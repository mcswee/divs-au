description: |
 My idea for New Hampshire uses the proposed state colors of red, orange and yellow. I take inspiration from the Second New Hampshire Regiment creating a saltire and cross in red over a field split between yelow and orange. In the center, nine stars taken in the same ring as they appear in the current flag represent the ninth state to join the Union.
