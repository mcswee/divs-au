 Wisconsin’s flag is currently a standard seal on a bedsheet. However, the crest contains a badger, which is a highly appropriate and established symbol for the state. My redesign moves away from the seal while keeping the black, white, and yellow colors. By evoking the stripes of a badger through a spiked pattern, the flag becomes fitting, unique, and visually distinctive.
