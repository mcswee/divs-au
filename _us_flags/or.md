description: |
 Oregon already possesses the foundation of an awesome, unique flag, but the design is currently undermined by a horrible seal on the front. I have turned the design around, making the beaver the primary symbol. To add further symbolism, I introduced green and gold stripes representing the beaches, the forests, and the legacy of the Oregon Trail. This reorganization elevates Oregon's most distinct feature, to a cohesive tribute to the state’s natural beauty and history.
