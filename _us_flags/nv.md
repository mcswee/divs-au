description: |
 Nevada’s existing flag is almost good, so I have kept the core design while removing the "Nevada" label from the emblem. To balance the composition, I enlarged the star to fill the resulting space.At the bottom of the flag, I added a gold, silver, and white bar. These stripes represent the state's mineral wealth and its identity as the Silver State, as well as the desert and snow as contrasting features of the state. These changes ground the design with a solid foundation that complements the traditional cobalt blue field.
