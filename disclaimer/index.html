Methodology & data sources

All electoral boundary data and division information are sourced directly from the Australian Electoral Commission (AEC) or Australian Parliament House (APH) website using publicly available GIS datasets. Geographic information, including Statistical Areas and suburb boundaries, are sourced from the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS).

Maps are created using QGIS. While every effort is made to ensure accuracy, the AEC remains the official source of truth for all electoral boundaries and voter information.
