            <h1>Tasmania redistribution 2025</h1>
            <p>The redistribution of Tasmania commenced on 12 August 2025. Public suggestions were sought from 8 October 2025 until 7 November 2025. Here you can find details of my suggestion to the Redistribution Committee.</p>
        </section>

        <section>
            <h2>Proposed electoral divisions</h2>
            <p>My suggestion ensures all electoral divisions in Tasmania meet the numerical thresholds for actual and projected enrolment, while adhering to community of interests criteria as outlined in the <em>Commonwealth Electoral Act 1918</em>.</p>
            <p>The numerical thresholds for this redistribution are:</p>

                <dt>Redistribution quota</dt>
                    <dd>Total electors: 413,176 divided by the number of divisions: 5 = 82,635</dd>

                    <dd>Between 74,372 and 90,898 actual electors</dd>
                <dt>Projected electoral quota</dt>
                    <dd>Total projected electors: 422,785 divided by the number of divisions: 5 = 84,557</dd>
                <dt>Threshold for projected electors within each division (Â±3.5%)</dt>
                    <dd>Between 81,598 and 87,526 projected electors</dd>

        <section>
            <h2>Summary of proposed changes</h2>
            <dl>
               <dt>Bass</dt>
                  <dd>Adds Prospect Vale. </dd>
               <dt>Braddon</dt>
                  <dd>No changes.</dd>
               <dt>Clatk</dt>
                  <dd>Adds Huon Valley and rest of Kingborough. Loses Glenorchy. </dd>
               <dt>Franklin</dt>
                  <dd>Abolished.</dd>
               <dt>Lyons</dt>
                  <dd>Loses Prospect Vale to Bass. Loses Gagebrook to West.</dd>
               <dt>West (new division)</dt>
                  <dd>Gains Clarence from Franklin, Glenorchy from Clark, and small part of Hobart, east of the Brooker Hwy.</dd>
            </dl>
