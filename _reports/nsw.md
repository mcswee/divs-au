There is no current redistribution for New South Wales. The most recent redistribution was due to start 26 February 2023, was deferred until 9 August 2023, due to the entitlement determination. The redistribution was determined on 10 October 2024. New South Wales lost one seat, North Sydney, bringing the total down to 46 seats.
