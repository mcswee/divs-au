Privacy policy

divs.au is committed to providing a high-quality spatial archive while respecting your privacy. This policy outlines how this site handles data and ensures transparency regarding the technical services used to render its maps and host the project.

Data collection

I do not collect, store, or sell any personal information. You can browse the entire archive anonymously. There are no user accounts, and I do not require your name, email address, or any other identifying details to use the site.

Hosting and infrastructure

This website is hosted using GitHub Pages. As part of this hosting service, GitHub may collect technical information from visitors, including web server logs and IP addresses, for security and service integrity.

The domain divs.au is registered through VentraIP. While VentraIP manages the domain registration, they do not collect data from your browsing session on this site.
