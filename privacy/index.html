Privacy policy

divs.au is a spatial archive dedicated to transparency and privacy. This policy outlines how data is handled and ensures clarity regarding the technical services used to render maps and host the project.

Data collection

I do not collect, store, or sell any personal information. You can browse the entire archive anonymously. There are no user accounts, and I do not require your name, email address, or any other identifying details to use the site.

Server logs and infrastructure

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most websites, the server automatically logs certain non-identifying information, such as your IP address, browser type, and the time of your visit. This information is used by the hosting provider to monitor site performance, maintain security, and ensure stability. Any data collected is subject to the GitHub Privacy Statement.

The domain divs.au is registered through VentraIP. While VentraIP manages the domain registration, they do not collect data from your browsing session on this site.

Third-party services

To provide interactive mapping and typography, this site relies on essential third-party libraries and assets. These services may process basic technical information (such as your IP address) to deliver content to your browser.

Leaflet.js & Map Tiles
Open-source libraries and external map providers (such as OpenStreetMap or Carto) used to display interactive maps.
Google Fonts
Web fonts used to provide consistent typography across the site.

Other libraries used on this site, such as PapaParse and TableSort, run entirely within your browser and do not transmit data to external servers.

This site contains links to external websites, including government archives and historical databases. I am not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third-party sites.
