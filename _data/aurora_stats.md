# AuRoRa Network Statistics

## Total Network
- **Total route kilometers:** 15,450 km (accounting for shared track sections, actual unique track ~14,200 km)
- **Total stations:** 101
- **Interchange hubs:** 22
- **Lines:** 10

## Key Journey Times (Express Services)

### Major Capital Connections
- Sydney → Melbourne: 3h 30m (880 km)
- Sydney → Brisbane: 3h 45m (920 km)
- Melbourne → Adelaide: 4h 00m (via Flinders, 980 km)
- Adelaide → Perth: 11h 00m (2,700 km) - overnight sleeper recommended
- Adelaide → Darwin: 12h 30m (3,000 km) - overnight sleeper recommended

### Regional Highlights

## Technical Specifications

### Rolling Stock
- **Maximum speed:** 350 km/h
- **Operating speed:** 300-320 km/h on dedicated HSR track
- **Average speed:** 220-250 km/h (including stops)
- **Train length:** 8-16 cars (200-400 seats)
- **Power system:** 25 kV AC overhead catenary

### Track Standards
- **Gauge:** Standard gauge (1,435 mm)
- **Minimum curve radius:** 4,000m (for 350 km/h operation)
- **Maximum gradient:** 3.5%
- **Track class:** Dedicated high-speed alignment, grade-separated

### Station Design
- **Island platforms:** 400m length (16-car trains)
- **Platform height:** 1,250mm (level boarding)
- **Through-running capability:** All major terminals
- **Interchange design:** Cross-platform transfers where possible

## Population Served

### Direct Service (within 5km of station)
- Approximately 18 million Australians
- ~72% of national population

### Catchment Area (within 50km of station)
- Approximately 21 million Australians
- ~84% of national population

## Shared Track Sections

Several sections serve multiple lines:
- **Sydney Central - Parramatta - Western Sydney Airport:** Federation + Barrier lines
- **Southern Cross - Sunshine:** Federation + Flinders lines
- **Keswick - Salisbury:** Barrier + Nullarbor + Territory lines
- **Roma Street Brisbane - Brisbane Airport:** Pacific + Capricorn + Outback lines
- **Bundaberg - Gladstone - Rockhampton:** Capricorn + Outback lines
- **Ballarat - Wangaratta:** Flinders + Inland lines

This shared infrastructure reduces total unique track requirements and enables efficient interchange operations.
