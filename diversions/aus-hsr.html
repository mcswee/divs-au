Australian High Speed Rail

This is my concept for a full-scale High Speed Rail project in Australia.

I propose a nation-spanning project across the country. It would serve every capital city (except obviously Hobart) and serve the major airports in most cities.

I'm naming it the AuRORa service, meaning Australian Regional Overland Rail. It comprises ten different lines.

  • Capital Line: connects Sydney - Canberra - Melbourne
  • Capricorn Line: connects Byron Bay - Gold Coast - Brisbane - Townsville - Cairns
  • Darling Line: connects Sydney - Broken Hill - Adelaide
  • Gulf Line: connects Toowoomba - Brisbane - Rockhampton - Mount Isa
  • Inland Line: connects Geelong - Bendigo - Albury - Parkes - Dubbo - Toowoomba
  • Nullarbor Line: connects Adelaide - Kalgoorlie - Perth
  • Outback Line: connects Adelaide - Alice Springs - Darwin
  • Pacific Line: connects Sydney - Newcastle - Coffs Harbour - Gold Coast - Brisbane
  • Southern Line: connets Adelaide - Ballarat - Melbourne - Traralgon
  • Western Line: connects Margaret River - Bunbury - Perth - Geraldton
