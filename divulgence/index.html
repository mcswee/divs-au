Methodology & Data Sources

All electoral boundary data and division information are sourced directly from the Australian Electoral Commission (AEC) using publicly available GIS datasets.

Geographic information including Statistical Areas, suburb boundaries and local government area boundaries are sourced from the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS).

While every effort is made to ensure the accuracy of the maps displayed, the AEC remains the official source of truth for all electoral boundaries and voter information.
