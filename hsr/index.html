Australian High Speed Rail

Project: AuRORa (Australian Regional Overland Rail)
CONCEPT OVERVIEW

A nation-spanning project connecting every mainland capital and major secondary hub. The AuRORa network is designed to integrate primary population centers with key regional airports through ten dedicated corridors.

NETWORK
CAPITAL Sydney – Canberra – Melbourne
PACIFIC Sydney – Newcastle – Coffs – Gold Coast – Brisbane
OUTBACK Adelaide – Alice Springs – Darwin
SOUTHERN Adelaide – Ballarat – Melbourne – Traralgon
NULLARBOR Adelaide – Kalgoorlie – Perth
WESTERN Margaret River – Bunbury – Perth – Geraldton
INLAND Geelong – Bendigo – Albury – Parkes – Dubbo
DARLING Sydney – Broken Hill – Adelaide
GULF Toowoomba – Brisbane – Rockhampton – Mount Isa
CAPRICORN Byron – Gold Coast – Townsville – Cairns
