South Australia redistribution 2025

The redistribution of South Australia commenced on 12 August 2025. Public suggestions were sought from 8 October 2025 until 7 November 2025. Here you can find details of my suggestion to the Redistribution Committee.

Proposed electoral divisions

My statewide suggestion ensures all electoral divisions in South Australia meet the numerical thresholds for actual enrolment and projected enrolment, while adhering to community of interests criteria as outlined in the Commonwealth Electoral Act 1918.

The numerical thresholds for this redistribution are:

Summary of proposed changes

Adelaide
Adds Marion and Unley LGA north of Cross Road. Loses part of Vale Park.
Barker
Adds Barossa LGA (and small part of Light LGA) from Spence.
Boothby
Adds Flagstaff Hill, loses all north of Cross Road.
Grey
Adds Light LGA from Spence.
Hindmarsh
No changes.
Kingston
Adds Aldinga and Sellicks Beach, loses Flagstaff Hill.
Makin
Adds Salisbury and Salisbury East.
Mayo
Loses Aldinga and Sellicks Beach.
Spence
Loses Salisbury, Salisbury East, Light LGA and Barossa LGA.
Sturt
Adds some of Vale Park.
