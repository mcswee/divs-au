Tasmania redistribution 2025

The redistribution of Tasmania commenced on 12 August 2025. Public suggestions were sought from 8 October 2025 until 7 November 2025. Here you can find details of my suggestion to the Redistribution Committee.

Proposed electoral divisions

My suggestion ensures all electoral divisions in Tasmania meet the numerical thresholds for actual and projected enrolment, while adhering to community of interests criteria as outlined in the Commonwealth Electoral Act 1918.

The numerical thresholds for this redistribution are:

Summary of proposed changes

Bass
Adds Prospect Vale.
Braddon
No changes.
Clatk
Adds Huon Valley and rest of Kingborough. Loses Glenorchy.
Franklin
Abolished.
Lyons
Loses Prospect Vale to Bass. Loses Gagebrook to West.
West (new division)
Gains Clarence from Franklin, Glenorchy from Clark, and small part of Hobart, east of the Brooker Hwy.
