what is this, actually

MusicBrainz indexes recording titles alphabetically-ish under the hood. Each pull picks a random one or two‑character title prefix (weighted toward how common that prefix roughly is in English), asks the index how many recordings match it, then asks for exactly one result at a random offset inside that match set.

That offset is the number ticking above  —  it's not decorative, it's the actual position in MusicBrainz's /ws/2/recording search index that got pulled. Same mechanism as reaching into a shelf of index cards and grabbing one at a random depth.

It isn't a perfectly uniform sample of all ~40 million recordings — prefixes with fewer entries are slightly overrepresented relative to their true share. But nothing here is ranked, scored, or filtered by how well-known it is. Cover art, where shown, comes from the Cover Art Archive and is often just missing.
