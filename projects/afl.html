The Model

Power Rating

Each team carries a cumulative rating that updates after every game. All teams start the season at zero — carry-over from prior seasons is used only to calibrate H2H and venue baselines, not the rating itself. This ensures Round 1 comparisons are fair regardless of bye timing.

Change Per Game

Δ = SurpriseComponent
+ MarginComponent
+ HundredBonus
+ TravelBonus
+ RestBonus

Surprise Factor

Surprise = 1 + tanh((OppTotal − TeamTotal) / 30), where Total = Rating + H2H + Venue + Rest. Beating a heavy favourite earns more; losing to the wooden spooners costs more. Surprise × Outcome = SurpriseComponent.

Win Probability

Prediction = 1 − (Surprise / 2). A team with Prediction >50% is the model's tip. Confident tips (>55%) have been correct in 2026 at a high rate.

H2H & Venue History

A rolling average of the last 11 results (win=+1, loss=−1, draw=0) against each opponent and at each venue. Baseline from 2002–2025; updated live each round.

Bonuses

Margin: Final margin ÷ 60 (negative if lost).
100-point: +0.2 if team scored 100+.
Travel: +0.2 if playing in a different state.
Rest: max(0, 7 − days since last game) × 0.2.
